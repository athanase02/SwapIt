Scenario: Sarah needs to borrow a projector from John

You'll experience the complete workflow from searching for an item to completing a transaction with ratings. This demonstrates how two SwapIt users interact throughout the entire borrowing process.

Step 1: Discover & Browse

Sarah searches for a projector on the browse page and finds John's listing.

Step 2: Send Request

Sarah clicks "View Details", fills in dates and message, and sends a borrow request.

Step 3: Receive & Approve

John logs in, sees the notification, reviews Sarah's request, and approves it.

Step 4: Communicate

Both users receive notifications and can message each other to arrange meeting details.

Step 5: Meet & Exchange

They meet at the agreed location, John hands over the projector, Sarah confirms pickup.

Step 6: Return & Rate

After use, Sarah returns the projector. Both users rate each other and leave reviews.
